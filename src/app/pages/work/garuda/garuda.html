Overview

Garuda is an advanced, real-time analytics platform for the Indian stock market, delivered as a cross-platform desktop application. I designed, built, and operate the entire system myself — frontend, backend, data pipeline, storage, security, visualization, and infrastructure.

It's a showcase of full-stack and systems engineering: how to ingest a fast live data stream, process it efficiently, store it as time-series, visualize it seamlessly, and secure and host it — all at very low cost.
